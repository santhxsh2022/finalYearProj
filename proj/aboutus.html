About Us & Why We Are The Best in Electric Wires

Wings Engineering, since 1980, a certified company, is a leading manufacturer of various types of wires and cables. The company has established a sound network of distributors and dealers to ensure good pre and after-sales services to our valued customers at very competitive prices. We offer a wide range of products like XLPE Cables, Power Cables, Control Cables, Domestic Wires & FRLS Wires. Our products are used extensively in different projects for corporate buildings, business centers, and malls in UAE, and exclusively in government departments like BHEL, NHPC, NTPC, Power Grid, etc.
